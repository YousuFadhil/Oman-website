      ALMUSTAQBAL HOTEL Apartments

      Located 3.2 km from Al Qanater, ALMUSTAQBAL HOTEL Apartments provides air-conditioned accommodation offering a patio, free Wi-Fi, and a shared lounge. Also, each unit has a kitchen, equipped with a microwave and refrigerator, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, ironing facilities, a desk and a seating area with a sofa. There is a private bathroom with a bidet, a hairdryer, and free toiletries in each unit. Guests at this aparthotel can enjoy an American breakfast. A car rental service is available at ALMUSTAQBAL HOTEL Apartments. The accommodation is 5.1 km from Musayyib and 5.4 km from Al Thabiti. The nearest airport is Muscat International Airport, 149 km away from ALMUSTAQBAL HOTEL Apartments.

    • 02

      Ibra Plaza Hotel

      Ibra Plaza Hotel is in Ibra and features a shared lounge area. Each accommodation at this 2-star hotel has view of the garden, and guests can enjoy use of the indoor pool. The accommodation includes a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and service Free Wi-Fi. This hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms fitted with a wardrobe, safety deposit box and flat-screen TV, as well as a private bathroom with a bidet. Bed linen and towels are featured in every room at Ibra Plaza Hotel.

    • 03

      SAJ Farm Inn

      Is in Ibra which is 160 km from Muscat International airport. The resort is built on a farm and occupies 26000-square meter of the farm. The resort is consists of 15 separate units (8 units as suite type with 2 bedrooms and hall and 7 in standard double bedrooms). The resort also includes water slides park for children and adults, mini-football ground, swimming pools; one is open for guest and members, while one swimming pool is for private booking, meeting room for 20 people, restaurant, and spacious outdoor areas.

    • 04

      Guest house tafadal

      Tafadal is in Ibra and features barbecue facilities. The property offers a shared lounge. The property has 3 bedrooms. Some rooms are equipped with a kitchen with a microwave. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the guest house. Staff are always available to help at reception. Guests can experience the authentic Omani lifestyle while staying at the property.

    • 05

      Golden Lights Hotel

      Golden Rays Hotel is in Ibra. This property features family rooms and a terrace for guests. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service, and free Wi-Fi throughout the property. A continental breakfast is available daily at the hotel.

    • 06

      Al Sharqiya Sands Hotel

      Offering an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Al Sharqiya Sands Hotel is in Ibra. and free WIFI service is available. All rooms feature a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a minibar. Complete with a dishwasher, it includes a dining area, fridge, and electric kettle. Featuring a bath or shower, a private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer. You will find a 24-hour front desk, a garden and a bar. Other facilities offered at the property include ticket service, tour desk and luggage storage. The property also offers free parking.

    • 07

      Byruha Ibra

      One of the best tourist and family camps in the state with an area of 10,500 square meters, as the camp contains many tourist attractions. In addition to the many luxurious hotel apartments that the camp contains, there are also various electronic games and others. There is also a swimming pool for children and adults inside the camp. Also, we do not forget the large restaurant that contains different and delicious foods and room delivery. It is necessary to order food at least 4 hours in advance. With free internet inside the camp and private parking.

    • 08

      Taif Hotel Oman

      Located in Ibra, Taif Amman Hotel features free Wi-Fi throughout the property. All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom.

      A wonderful place to hold events, where friends meet and some of them play cards and other games.

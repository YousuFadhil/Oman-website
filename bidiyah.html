      Desert Nights Camp

      Desert Nights Camp is located in Wahiba Sands/Sharqiyah Sands, 2 hours drive from Muscat. This secluded area covers 11 km of beautiful desert landscape. Many services are offered to guests free of charge, including driving to the sand dunes in a 4x4 vehicle to watch the sunset with snacks and soft drinks served during the ride. Camel rides are also available inside the camp in the morning, and transportation services are available between Al Wasil City and Desert Nights Camp at an additional cost for travelers traveling with vehicles not suitable for driving in the desert.

    • 02

      Starry Domes Desert Camp

      A luxury dome desert camp in the heart of the Sharqiyah sands. It is a place of golden sand dunes where you can enjoy watching the sunrise and stars from your bed. It is consists of three luxury domes with a private sitting area per dome. You can enjoy a Zip line ride and an extraordinary sunset scene reflected at the mirrors to carry exceptional memories forever.

    • 03

      Oriental Nights Rest House

      Get your trip off to a great start with a stay at this property, which offers car park free of charge. Conveniently situated in the Bidiyah part of A'Sharqiyah Sands (Wahiba), this property puts you close to attractions and interesting dining options. Restaurant and outdoor pool are among the special facilities that will enhance your stay with on-site convenience

    • 04

      Crescent Dessert Camp

      Desert Private Camping Company corporation with different Glamping and Traditional Bedouin setups and is giving corporate clients a professional marketing, consulting and managing services for private events and setups.

    • 05

      Desert Retreat Camp

      Located in Bidiyah, Desert Rose Camp provides accommodation with pool views, a restaurant, an outdoor swimming pool and a shared lounge. There is a fully equipped private bathroom that includes a bidet and free toiletries. Continental and buffet breakfast options are available every morning at this camping. Desert Rose Camp offers a children's playground. Ski equipment hire is available at the property and skiing and cycling can be enjoyed nearby. Desert Rose Camp is located 48 km from Ibra and 10 km from Hawiyah.

    • 06

      Jawharat Bidiyah Resort

      Located in the Al Ghubi district, 7 km from Al Ghubi, Jawharat Bidiah Resort provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, free bike access, and an outdoor swimming pool. Bidiyah is 7.5 km from the property, while Al Jahiz is 8.1 km away. It features a fitness center and a garden. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests. Rooms are air-conditioned and have a safety deposit box and a flat-screen TV. Some units at the resort have a balcony. Guests at Jawharat Bidiah Resort "JBR can enjoy a continental or an American breakfast. The property offers a children's playground. You can play billiards and table tennis at Jawharat Bidiah Resort "JBR", and the area is popular for cycling.

    • 07

      ALMOROUJ FARM INN

      boasting a garden, terrace and views of mountain, Almorouj Farm inn - Bidiya is situated in Badīyah, 4.1 km from Al Wāşil. The property is around 8.3 km from Jāḩis, 10 km from Al Ghabbī and 10 km from Al Mintirib. The accommodation features room service and a tour desk for guests. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a wardrobe, a kettle, a fridge, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bidet. All rooms at Almorouj Farm inn - Bidiya are fitted with a seating area. A buffet, Asian or halal breakfast is available each morning at the property. Speaking Arabic, English and Hindi, staff are ready to help around the clock at the reception. Shāhiq is 12 km from the accommodation, while Al Raka is 13 km from the property. The nearest airport is Muscat International Airport, 191 km from Almorouj Farm inn - Bidiya.

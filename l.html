    • 01

      SAMA Al Areesh

      SAMA Al Areesh is a beautiful camp set in the middle of the Wahiba Sands. The property is placed right on the edge of the sand dunes, you can easily walk up the dunes and feel the awe of the place. The camp itself is simple, but has all the amenities you will need, delicious food, hot showers warm comfy rooms and a lovely outdoor communal seating area. The location is good as it is easily accessible by road. The camp offers a range of activities including sand boarding, dune bashing and camel rides. The camp is not that far off the beaten track but you do still get the desert experience and 100% authentic. Rooms are quite specious and like individual cottage with attached bathroom.

    • 02

      Almderb Park Tourism

      Al Mudareb rest house is very beautiful, tidy, organized, and clean. It contains various games for children, a tartan playground, a billiard hall, and other games, as well as places equipped for slaughtering, grilling, and foaling. There are sleeping rooms, as well as swimming pools for children and men, and a closed pool for women.

    • 03

      Sandglass Camp

      A simple camp owned by a simple local person who enjoys tranquility in the middle of the sand dunes with a beautiful view of the sunrise and sunset. It is possible to turn off the lights after eating dinner and enjoy watching the stars and sipping tea under the open sky
